Filter Definitions

Feed ready — Publicly priced instruments with accessible APIs or data feeds. Switchboard can pull from Bloomberg, Alpaca, EMMA, exchange feeds, fund company APIs, etc.
Partial — Data exists somewhere but requires custom data source configuration by the builder.
Not feasible — Not oracle-able due to: legal non-transferability, dead/obsolete markets, pure tax instruments, individualized instruments, or structurally non-standardizable assets.
Privacy Override — Sensitive or confidential underlying data that should be kept private from the oracle operator and on-chain observers. Needs Data Feed Variable Overrides to preserve privacy.
